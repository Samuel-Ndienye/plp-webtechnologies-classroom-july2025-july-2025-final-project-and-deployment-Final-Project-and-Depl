Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How much does it cost to join FarmConnect?

+

Basic farmer registration is completely free. We only charge a small transaction fee when you make a sale through our platform.

How do I get paid for my products?

+

Payments are processed securely through our platform. You can choose to receive payments via bank transfer, PayPal, or check.

What types of products can I sell?

+

You can sell fresh produce, grains, dairy products, livestock, and other agricultural products. All products must meet our quality standards.

Do you provide transportation?

+

We partner with logistics companies to help coordinate transportation. We can help arrange pickup and delivery of your products to buyers.
